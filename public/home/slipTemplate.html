For Home/Hotel testing, kindly note:

  • The service provider will call you within 1 hour of the time of booking to schedule the time for the medical team to meet you at your home or hotel to collect the PCR test sample.

  • For Urgent or Express Service types, our service provider will endeavour to deploy the medical team within 2 hours of the call to schedule the appointment (subject to availability).

  • You can contact the service provider team at 04-230-0500 if you are unable to wait for their call to schedule the timing of the medical team's visit to your location.

  • Once the sample is collected, the test result will be released by email to the customer as per the category booked (test is processed from the time of sample collection, not from the time of booking).

  • Home/Hotel testing is currently covers Dubai only.
